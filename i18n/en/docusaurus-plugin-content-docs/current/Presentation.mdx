## And what about the team?

Once you've relayed the mascot, you're part of the "Trace across the world" team. You can return to the site regularly to follow its progress around the world.

If you know anyone in the Trace area, you can put them in touch with the carrier via the team's [WhatsApp](https://chat.whatsapp.com/FKwW0q23yVk3OdKNKAxGnE) group. Perhaps this will enable us to find a future relay worker to take Trace a little further towards its next stage.

Finally, as a carrier, don't hesitate to send us some photos/videos of you with Trace on the <a href="https://www.instagram.com/traceacrosstheworld/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Instagram account</a> at places close to where you are.
